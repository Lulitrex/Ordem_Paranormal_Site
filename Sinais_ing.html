Signs from the other side

Synopsis

On an isolated island, there is an enormous mansion, built by a painter who mysteriously disappeared over 20 years ago. An art appraiser is hired to spend a few days inside this mansion, searching for valuable paintings within it. To do this, he decides to bring his family on the trip. After all, what could go wrong?

Spoilers about the season

Main events

The group of five receives a call from Morato, their leader and mentor, to investigate disappearances linked to Alheios. They discover that Morato has disappeared and investigate Granja Abobrinha, where he was last seen. They find paranormal signs, VHS tapes with messages from Morato, and strange symbols. While investigating the treehouse, they uncover a laboratory with documents about Alheios and a paranormal larva. Watching recordings, they identify a hooded intruder and a mysterious black van. Morato warns them about the treehouse. At the farm, they suspect Adágio as the host of a parasite. They attempt to expel the parasite, revealing a creature. The Five confront the Interfloral parasite in Adágio, resulting in an explosion. The creature resurfaces and grabs Guizo, transporting him to a mysterious vision. Xande and Lírio face the Interfloral and kill it. Upon analyzing Adágio, they discover that the parasite was keeping him alive.

Don, Adágio's son, reveals the connection between aliens and the lake's inhabitants, showing a video to Morato. The Five investigate a bonfire, encounter alien enthusiasts, and explore an abandoned station with suspicious information. They discover the presence of the Fummu parasite. In an ambush at the Transmission Station, they are surprised by the generator's explosion, facing the Fummu, resulting in the creature's death and serious injuries to Lírio. Guizo witnesses strange activities at the camp, capturing images of people being placed in a van. While investigating the camp, they discover suspicious marks, Cannabis herbs, and an SOS signal from the other side of the lake. Chico, analyzing a computer, identifies a triangulation between the transmission tower, the house at Granja Abobrinha, and an unknown point. Dara, hearing radio noises, enters a trance and receives mysterious messages about her singularity. The group decides to return to the farm to rescue Don.

As they arrive, they find Adágio's grave open and the house dark, except for a TV displaying hypnotic symbols. Don warns about the black van and the change in the family members' attitudes. Guizo retrieves tapes from the house, revealing Morato's messages about the danger of the TV and the presence of the men in suits. In the treehouse, Chico initiates a strange conversation with a terminal, revealing to be Voytek, the Memoflígico. The group recalls Voytek's tragedy in the sewer. Lírio, Dara, Xande, and Guizo head to the van, while Chico continues interacting with Voytek. A flashback reveals Voytek causing conflicts and doubts about his death. Communication between Chico and Voytek intensifies, leading to the onset of Chico's madness. The group faces the Memoflígico, destroying the computer and freeing Chico. An ambush by the men in suits occurs, and the group witnesses a strange event in the sky, and "HE" arrives, but they forget and wake up, not knowing who Dara is.

Upon finding Calisto, the group is alerted to the mission of saving Dara and Morato. In the office, Arnaldo reveals the involvement of the Order, and the group witnesses a suspicious meeting involving familiar figures. Chico discovers Dara's location in the transmission tower of TV Varminho, and the group prepares for the final mission. The team, consisting of Xande, Chico, Guizo, and Lírio, confronts challenges in the TV Varminho Tower to rescue Dara and Morato. They infiltrate the broadcasting station, face security personnel and producers under influence, and discover capsules containing people from the parameter list, including Dara. After releasing Morato from one of the capsules, the group readies themselves for the next phase of the mission, determined to save their comrades and confront the hidden challenges within the tower.

The Four meet Morato on the fourth floor of the Impossible City. They rescue Jerson and Vicente from the capsules using specific passwords. During their exploration, they encounter a Rastropoda. After the battle, they uncover information about the TV Varminho Project and continue their search. On the fifth floor, they face armed men in suits. In a peculiar elevator, they reach a disproportionately sized floor. Guizo interacts with a computer, discovering armed men and triggering a chase. After overcoming challenges and making discoveries, they reach the sixth floor and find a room with capsules containing people linked to a greater purpose. Translating symbols, they open the capsules but face a threatening entity. Morato sacrifices himself, Xande is corrupted, and the remaining three confront choices and revelations in the pursuit of the Passage. Guizo decides to enter, while Chico and Lírio return to the normal world. Dara also chooses to return, facing the consequences of what they discovered in the Impossible City.
