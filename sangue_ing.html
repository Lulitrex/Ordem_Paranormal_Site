Blood

It all begins with Blood. Blood is the flow that bathes the eternity of the Other Side.
This element is related to extreme emotions such as pain, obsession, passion, love, hunger, and hatred. The colors representing Blood are shades of red. The behavior of the Blood entity seems to devour all available flesh and blood in Reality, causing the maximum pain in the most extreme and intense way possible.
"Blood is the entity of feeling. It seeks intensity: pain, obsession, passion, love, hunger, hatred everything involving feeling an extreme emotion pleases the Blood entity."
The extreme feelings of Blood surpass the reason and calmness of Knowledge.

The Throne

The Throne is described as a solid physical form that transforms its surroundings into an environment of carnage, monstrosities, and obsession. Until now, only the illustration of this Relic is available. Like the other Relics of Calamity, its origin and first bearer are unknown, only that it has been in reality "since the beginning" and can force itself onto its next bearer if it deems them "worthy" to be its "heir." The process of the bearer's transformation into the Devil is not known, but unlike the other Relics, its bearer can converse and act independently of the Devil. The bearer can offer or accept a "Pact"—an agreement capable of generating an obsessive dependence, resulting in a mutual exchange between the involved figures. To perform this pact, a symbol known as the "Mark of the Pact" is required.

Devil

This entity of Blood, complemented by Knowledge and Fear, is described as one of the most significant manifestations of the Other Side in the history of Reality. It is someone who has never been defeated, controlled, or imprisoned, capable of invoking and controlling any paranormal creatures associated with Blood in Reality. References to this malevolent figure are present in various legends and cultures within Reality. Everyone has heard of the Devil, but only a few have had the sensation of displeasure and agony of meeting him. The Devil is constantly mentioned as an entity that can be conjured through a ritual, offering gifts to those who forge pacts with the creature, with the payment being demanded later.

Blood Creatures

Enpap-X

The Enpap-X is a paranormal creature of Blood complemented by Knowledge, emerging from pain and torture. It can manifest when a person is forced to live chained and imprisoned, exposed to various brutal and paranormal events incessantly, unable to end their life during the torment. Initially, this reddish creature possesses the same characteristics as a Muscular Exist, being extremely strong and resistant. When transformed, the Enpap-X glows in a vibrant red. Its texts light up, just like its right eye. Moreover, this monstrosity acquires a colossal and extremely resistant physical stature, its four large arms become even more muscular, and its hand takes on a more grotesque and aggressive appearance, with long claws at the tips of its fingers.
