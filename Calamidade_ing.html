Calamity

Synopsis

Six months have passed since Kian's return. No sign of any interference with the balance. However, a new lead prompts Ordo Realitas to act once again. An old case file from Arnaldo Fritz, detailing an entity described as "The Devil," assembles a new team of agents. The team includes the sharp and clever Rubens Naluti, the retired agent Antonio Pontevedra, the promising Carina Leone, daughter of the leader of an Italian mafia of paranormal agents, and the veterans Arthur Cervero and Dante. Together, they must delve deeper into the case while continuing the quest for the power of the Relics of Calamity.

Spoilers about the season

Main events

The Vultures Team analyzes Arnaldo Fritz's Grimoire, uncovering blocked information. They explore new pages with illustrations of the Devil and details about the Masks Sect. Balu identifies the crest of the Leone Family, prompting Carina to call her father about possible documents at Leone Mansion. With the trip to Italy approved, they face Blood Zombies and discover a monster in the vault. Analyzing the recordings, they identify Kian and Scribes leading an attack. They confront the Devil, resulting in a Blood Zombie invasion. Using turrets and rituals, they face the horde, discovering Rana's transformation into a Bestial Blood Zombie. After winning, they find invitations to Kian's game. Veríssimo decides to go to Italy with the new improvised base.

The Scribes, led by Kian, invade Leone Mansion, facing fierce resistance from the family. After defeating members, they discover Enzo Leone locked in a fridge, and upon defeating him, they find documents about Leone transfers to Brazil. Attempting to open the vault, Kian kills Marco Leone. Joui Jouki seeks revenge, massacres the Scribes, expresses hatred, and vows to pursue them to hell. Amidst the chaos, Rana tries to open another portal, but Joui kills her. The remaining Scribes flee through the portal, leaving behind a trail of destruction and revenge.

The Vultures Team faces paranormal challenges after discovering the existence of the Masks Sect and the Symbol of Calamity. They find Arnaldo Fritz's grimoire, revealing connections with the sect. During investigations, they detect a hooded figure in the Colosseum and confront Vinicio Ludos, a follower of the Host, triggering a battle. While exploring, they encounter members of the Ordo Realitas, documents about the Ordo Calamitas, and the "Semper Clausa" base. After a brutal confrontation, they identify Joui Jouki, a former ally. Amidst discoveries about masks related to the governors, they confront followers of the Host, solve puzzles, and access the Colosseum's underground.

In Semper Clausa, upon touching statues in an ancient room, they are transported to the past, controlling members of the Ordo Calamitas. Gaius is chosen by the Calamity Relic, transforming into the entity of Chaos. Upon their return, suspicions about Gaius's connection to the Mask of Despair arise. The team faces more paranormal challenges, discovers marks, and is summoned to games by the entity of Chaos. The games continue until only Celestine and Amalia remain, competing amidst the chaos. Dante initiates the transcendence process, revealing connections with Arnaldo Fritz, the man on the throne associated with the Devil and the Host. The Magistrate indicates the need for Arnaldo's golden clock as a relic to stop Kian, ending the scene with the team facing the Devil and the Host.

Kian's marked team is teleported to the Santa Menefreda Orphanage, where Kian reveals his return. After heading to the Domus Aurea, connected to Semper Clausa, they face blood zombies and a Cyclops. Solving a puzzle, they confront the Minotaur and obtain the key to proceed. Arriving at the Church of Blood, they face the band Metallic Dragons, leading to an intense battle. T-Bag kills the remaining members after confrontations and revelations. Kian appears, under orders, the scribes leave the room, except for Gal, who is stabbed by the Calamity Director as part of his plan to betray the Ordo Realitas. Gal is left alone with Clarissa Leão, revealing a betrayal in progress.

The Vultures Team faces Arnaldo Fritz, now the Host, and the Devil. Arnaldo shows a video of Gal being tortured by Clarissa and killed by Kian. The Host offers the choice to save or let Gal die. Arthur presses the button to save Gal, but Joui tries to stop him. Gal is taken by a pink flame. The Masks Sect appears, and the Vultures team allies with them to confront the Host in the ruins of the Ordo Calamitas. The Host transforms, multiplying into the forms of the ancient governors of the Colosseum. The team fights against the Hosts, with Balu and Liber exchanging weapons. The Magistrate has lost part of the Masks Sect and helps in the shadows. The team faces the true form of the Host, an Energy Relic. After an intense battle, Joui kills the Host, now possessing the Energy Relic.

The Vultures team and other agents of the Ordo Realitas face Kian in the Colosseum. Kian kills the Magistrate and turns against his marked ones, but T-Bag, Damir, and Boris decide to join the Vultures. Veríssimo uses a hidden truth to hold Kian back, and Carina completes the Calamity Grimoire, nullifying Kian's rituals. Joui engraves the death kanji on Kian, but Kian kills him. Arthur arrives at the Colosseum, witnessing Joui's last moments and places a black box near Kian, while the battle continues with Kian attacking everyone. Rubens hides a mysterious ring in the box, which Dante discovers is dangerous. Balu and Arthur bring down Kian, and, seizing the opportunity, put the ring on his finger, imprisoning him in robes and a helmet. Kian, the Calamity Director, is finally contained.
