Paranormal Order

Synopsis

The story began around agents of the secret organization Ordo Realitas, who were in charge of investigating a mysterious school, a source of strong evidence of paranormal activity following an unexplained fire. Once there, the team Investigators discover not only brutal murders and grotesque monsters, but a cult that, together with school staff, used the students themselves as guinea pigs for experiments, in attempt to create new monstrous beings and further weaken the membrane.

The Agents of the Order investigate the Nostradamus School and upon analyzing the place they discover that it was a planned fire, and that Gabriel Opspor is connected, and by connection they go after the former teacher Alexsander Kothe. When they confront Alex, they discover his dismissal and disturbing flashes. Alex is invited to officially join. During the investigation of Gonzales' car, Daniel finds Matheus Geleerd's file, revealing that Gabriel murdered Agatha in the bunker, resulting in the creation of Degolificado. After Confronting them, Liz kills Matias, neutralizes Olavo and captures Gabriel.
