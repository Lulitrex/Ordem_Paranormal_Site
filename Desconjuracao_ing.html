Unconjuration

Synopsis

In "Unbinding," the Order's agents are on a mission to uncover the mysteries of a cult that has not only made one of their agents disappear but has also taken numerous other homeless individuals, all at the behest of a "larger group." Thus, the team engages in a battle against various occult branches to rescue their agent and safeguard their own reality from a threat much greater than anticipated.

Spoilers about the season

Main events

The team investigates Elisapet's apartment, discovering that she visited an orphanage and ultimately faces a Beast. Joui and the others handle the situation, deciding to take Tristan back to the Order's base due to his injuries. While Arthur and Kaiser continue their investigation, they confront a supposed occultist, Jorge, who reveals his true nature and is executed by Joui. The group reunites at the base, where Beatrice and Fernando reveal the truth about Luciano's identity. Back at the building, they face occultists and Blood Zombies, uncovering a sinister ritual. Erin experiences paranormal insanity and is severely injured. Sidney, the building manager, performs a ritual, leading to an intense confrontation. Erin faints on the brink of death. Joui and Kaiser deal with remaining zombies. The team faces challenges, internal conflicts, and surprising revelations during the occult mission.

The team encounters Dante, a former acquaintance of Beatrice, in the Order's prison. Dante doesn't remember her but reveals his past at the Santa Menefreda Orphanage and his involvement with occultists to save a child. While sharing information about Denis Batista, the group discovers the attempted sacrifice to save Fernando, resulting in Denis' death. Luciano, now controlling Fernando's body, reveals his selfish intentions, leading to internal conflicts within the group. Dante, Joui, and Kaiser interrogate Nathaniel, a prisoner with a death mark, but obtain few answers. The team prepares to investigate the Orphanage, led by Dante. At the Orphanage, they discover blind occultists, face Existed beings, and find enigmatic messages. A letter reveals Gal's rebellion and mentions familiar names like Lilian and Leonardo Gomes.

During the exploration, the team faces Existed beings, unravels puzzles, and confronts a group of scribes led by Henri. They encounter Anthony's challenges related to the elements Blood, Death, Blood, and Energy, discovering that Liz is on the second floor. To go up, they need a key and go through other challenges to obtain it, accessing the second floor. At the end of the corridor, they come across Elizabeth Webber. The Order's team rescues Liz. As they descend to the first floor, they encounter the Decollated, a monster familiar to Liz. In an intense escape, Dante transcends using a ritual learned from Nina. They face Existed beings and come across a hooded figure, Leonardo Gomes, and Gal, who controls Joui and kills Liz, revealing that the Unbinding is not yet over.

The team gathers and sets out to investigate the "Cursed Mansion" in Canadense. As they explore the location, they find signs of suspicious activities. During the investigation, they split into groups, discovering an old diary revealing details about a former resident and exploring a secret room with human remains. An audio tape reveals rumors about the house being haunted. When they attempt to burn photos to uncover secrets, Kaiser has a disturbing experience seeing a macabre smile in a black photo. An invisible creature attacks, levitating Luciano, but suddenly stops, and the photos return to normal. Examining other images, they find Sumerian cuneiform writings and a statue of Leonardo Gomes. The investigation culminates in a room with black candles and coffins, revealing a macabre atmosphere.

O episódio na Mansão Endiabrada começa com a descoberta de quatro caixões, revelando fotos e uma fita de áudio com um homem delirante. Ao tentar revelar as fotos, encontram estágios de decomposição e um símbolo na tampa do caixão. Kaiser recobra memórias, descobrindo um cristal hexagonal. Em um confronto com o Viajante, Beatrice é atacada e se torna uma Existed. Joui mata o monstro, mas a equipe luta para reverter a condição de Beatrice. Dante transcende e aprende o ritual de leitura psíquica. Kaiser coloca o cristal hexagonal no caixão, revelando Vanessa Ângelo. O grupo aproveita para todos transcenderem, melhorando suas forças. Resolvendo o enigma, a porta da sala de fotografias se abre, onde encontram um quarto com bonecas antigas, um guarda-roupa entreaberto e uma cadeira de balanço, encerrando o episódio.

Thiago takes them to Sovaco Seco, where they uncover information about occultist suspects. In the bar, Arthur Cervero stands out as the lively guitarist of the band. Cesar receives an enigmatic message, and the team gets distracted while Thiago takes time to talk to Getúlio. When they go after Thiago, they discover Getúlio's body and a Spawned Spider, facing the creature and finding a trail. Following the slime trail, they find Thiago unconscious and a giant Aracnasite. In the battle, Cristopher sacrifices himself to save Thiago, resulting in his cruel death. Elizabeth explodes the giant spider, setting the forest on fire. Thiago survives but bids farewell to Cristopher, whose body is devoured by the spiders.

At the Cursed Mansion, the agents face the challenge of doors with colored locks. Upon turning the black key, they notice red footprints leading to the bathroom. While investigating, Dante uses his ritual and visualizes a silhouette drowned in the bathtub, connecting it to the tale "The Drowned Woman" from the book "The Observer." Following the book's instructions, they perform rituals to close the valve and turn on the faucets, resulting in the pool being filled with blood, and the battle beginning. Despite difficulties, the team prevails, and the creature vomits a key, and everyone recovers. As they advance to the last door, they find Frederico Mare's room, discovering his tragic past connected to the house. Joui asks to go to the cemetery, where he bids farewell to the group and becomes part of the Masks Sect.

After Joui joins the Masks Sect, the team returns to the Ordo Realitas base. Everyone transcends, acquiring new abilities and purchasing cursed items from Agatha. Upon arriving in Canadense, they encounter a horrifying scene, with the Gatekeeper dead and the Enpap transformed into a creature. In an intense battle, aided by Bruno, they face Henri. Upon winning, Henri threatens suicide to kill three marked individuals, leading to the decision to let him go. With Bruno, they explore the sewers of the Cursed Mansion, uncovering occult secrets, facing Sukkal and Scribes, and solving puzzles that reveal details about Kian and the Marked. The battle culminates in Sukkal's transformation, Erin goes insane, Anthony dies, and is thrown into a rift. The paranormal exposure reaches 50%, and Arthur and Kaiser transcend.

Upon reaching the Orphanage, they discover the shocking scene of Gal manipulating Tim and revealing the execution of Beatrice, Orpheu, Tristan, and Elizabeth, initiating the battle. At the height of the battle, Gal reveals Kian's true plan, an ancient occultist and manipulator behind the events. The goal is to perform the Unbinding to prevent the existence of the paranormal. Luciano, formerly known as Fernando, discovers his true nature and begins the process of transcending, revealing himself as a vessel for Kian. The team faces Gal and Kian in an intense battle, with significant sacrifices. The Masks Sect intervenes, taking the survivors to safety but leaving Kian and Gal behind. Kaiser, in an act of sacrifice, uses his last ritual to save his friends, then being disintegrated by Kian. The enigmatic hacker, known as the Angel of the Night, leaves their final words as their existence is erased.
