Energy

Chaos is inevitable.
This element is characterized by everything that cannot be explained, the intangible, anarchy, and transformation. One of the most striking manifestations of this element is in an ethereal, ghostly form, described as a matter that is in constant transformation between solid, liquid, and gaseous states, taking on an appearance of "liquid flame" in colors representing Energy shades of purple, blue, cyan, green, and pink. The behavior of this element is instability itself, seeking only Chaos.
"Energy is the entity of chaos. Everything that cannot be explained, the intangible, anarchy. Constant change, heat and cold, light and darkness. Anything involving unpredictability and transformation pleases the Energy entity."
The transformation of Energy overloads the effects of Death.

Energy Relic

While the Energy Relic is not in the presence of a bearer or object, it takes on a non-solid, ethereal form, resembling a ray or an infinite flame, maintaining constant movement and transformation. There is a distorted aura around it, as if Reality struggles to contain this physical presence that unfolds in waves, attempting to stay in constant transformation. This reaction of the Relic becomes even stronger in the presence of the Marked. Like the other Relics of Calamity, it is known only that it has been in reality "since the beginning," choosing its heralds for thousands of years at extremely opportune moments in their lives. When it finds a bearer, the Energy Relic merges with an object near them, subsequently taking control of that individual and causing every atom forming the molecular structure of their existence to vibrate and distort. This results in the bearer's mind being invaded by a wave of personalities and overrun by different and inconsequential thoughts.

Host

This Energy entity with a complement of Knowledge and Fear is one of the most incessant manifestations throughout the history of Reality. It seeks only to revel in agony and Chaos, distorting the rules of Reality. No one can control everything. Reality needs to be transformed by improbability. The Host is a manifestation that attaches itself to humans, engaging in various games with rules that are extremely unpredictable and even beyond its control. Despite its immeasurable power, many have survived its games, but not without marks of these gruesome events, especially in their minds. The games of the Host are sadistic, cruel, and utterly twisted. Additionally, the Host seems to have a chaotic and distorted chronological perception, with anachronistic events associated with it throughout history. For example, in the Colosseum of Rome, some rulers from history were its bearers in their respective eras.

Energy Creatures

Sukkalgir

The Sukkalgir is a paranormal creature of Energy with a complement of Knowledge, arising from torture through flames in ancient Sumeria. Its origin involves a maddening process that marked the victim's skin with the horrific deeds of its captor, using fire as a torture element. Initially, Sukkalgir has the same characteristics as an Existing one. During its transformation, reality distorts, and the ambient light is drawn into its body. When transformed, its body takes on a form of Energy and Chaos, resembling flames in a greenish hue. Sukkalgir is enveloped in a luminous aspect, with texts spread across its body that light up, moving and floating.
