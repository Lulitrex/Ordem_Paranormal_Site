Felps

Felipe Zaghetti, better known as Felps, is a content creator and streamer. He played "O Ajudante" in the 9th episode of "O Segredo na Floresta" and portrayed Rubens Naluti in the 7th episode of "Desconjuração" as special appearances. Later, he returns as Rubens in "Ordem Paranormal: Calamidade," now part of the main cast, and also plays Amalia in the games of the Host. Felps also portrayed Guilherme "Guizo" R. Santos in "Sinais do Outro Lado."

Characters

Helper

The Helper had gray hair with a shaved pompadour on the sides, resembling others in the village of Santo Berço, gray skin, completely black eyes, and black stripes on the face, forming something similar to the letter F. The Ajudante was optimistic and cheerful like those who live in Santo Berço, showing great happiness with his city and pride for his master, Ferreiro. He was killed by the Porteiro at the end of the episode in which he appeared.
