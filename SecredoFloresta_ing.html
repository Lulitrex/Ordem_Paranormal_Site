Secret in the forest

Synopsis

The Reality Order team gathers at the Alpha Tower in São Paulo with the enigmatic Mr. Veríssimo. Joui Jouki, Cristopher Cohen, Cesar Oliveira, Thiago Fritz, and Elizabeth Webber form the group. Veríssimo reveals that the Kelvin Team disappeared while investigating a peculiar murder case with an occult symbol in Carpazinha, Rio Grande do Sul. On a flight to Carpazinha, the team is determined to unravel the mystery involving Miguel Cariad, Mariana Larona, and Kenan Thomas.

Spoilers about the season

Main events

The team explores the Little Carp Sanatorium, uncovering evidence of grotesque experimental treatments, including electric shocks and prohibited drugs. After facing Blood Beast Zombies and humanoids in the storage room, they discover numbered keys and unlock one of the rooms containing documents about "Patient 71" and improper nurses. Upon fixing a panel, they confront an Electric Aberration, resulting in an intense battle. They investigate the past of Patient 71, using the Aberration's body to open a door. While exploring cells, they hear a melody and encounter a black larva formed by two bodies, causing adverse mental effects.

Thiago leads them to Sovaco Seco, where they uncover information about occultist suspects. In a bar, Arthur Cervero stands out as the lively guitarist of the band. Cesar receives an enigmatic message, and the team gets distracted while Thiago takes time to talk to Getúlio. When they go after Thiago, they discover Getúlio's body and a Spawned Spider, facing the creature and finding a trail. Following the slime trail, they find Thiago unconscious and a giant Aracnasite. In the battle, Cristopher sacrifices himself to save Thiago, resulting in his cruel death. Elizabeth explodes the giant spider, setting the forest on fire. Thiago survives but bids farewell to Cristopher, whose body is devoured by the spiders.

The group of protagonists explores a macabre mansion and encounters a series of supernatural events. During the investigation, they discover documents and diaries related to mysterious activities, including a project called SB. They face zombies, gang members, and finally confront the Black Death Ghoul. The battle is intense, resulting in tragedies such as the deaths of Gregório and Brúlio. The surviving group faces emotional challenges, with Cesar and Thiago being controlled by the Ghoul, causing harm to their friends. Despite the victory over the creature, the mansion remains a dark and somber setting marked by the loss of lives.

The group decides to explore a cave, following the map provided by Lurdete and Ana Vitória. They arrive at Santo Berço, an isolated town, where they are welcomed by the friendly Gatekeeper. The Blacksmith, the community leader, invites them to the Trial, a local event. Suspicious, they head to the Forest, where they witness the conversion of Murilo, the former Hotel employee. In the Tavern, Joui succumbs to the city's influence, while Liz remains resistant. They visit the Farm and are intrigued by a legendary labyrinth at the village center. Thiago and Joui explore the maze and realize its illogical nature. As they exit, a fog emerges, signaling "The Night." They face malevolent creatures, resulting in the death of the Assistant. Upon returning to the Blacksmith's Forge, they find his body with the Spiral Symbol drawn in blood, indicating a dark event.

At the Santo Berço Farm, the team investigates creatures attacking animals and discovers a mysterious symbol. They plan an ambush in the barn to capture the creature. During the Night, they discover a wild Lucidios in the hollow tree. Arthur tends to Jennifer, the injured cow, while the group sets out to retrieve green crystals from the Doctor's house. The next morning, they investigate the Tower and suspect the Gatekeeper as the murderer. They find clues of Lucidios and blood-stained rags in his bed. Joui alerts the Blacksmith about the discoveries, but the Blacksmith is called to the Forest, where they find the Collector dead on the Blacksmith's own trunk. The group is guided to the cave to check the Door.

The team led by the Miner explores the Santo Berço Cave in search of the sealed door. They face creatures like Mud Moles and Batwings, sustaining minor injuries. While confronting a Labra in the gallery of red crystals, Thiago is injured, but they defeat the creature. In the gallery of blue crystals, they encounter the Succ, which attacks Thiago, threatening his life until Cesar destroys it. Upon reaching the sealed door, Liz uses the Old Fool's black card, revealing a Spiral Symbol and an unknown combination. Guided by the Miner, they explore another part of the cave with black crystals, where they discover the Gatekeeper hidden. The Miner attacks Cesar by surprise, revealing his Lucidios form, and the group faces the giant. Thiago saves Cesar, killing the Miner. The Gatekeeper reveals secrets about Santo Berço, explaining his desire to destroy the entity controlling the community. The group decides to help and opens the door.

In the bunker, they find a model of Santo Berço and documents by Eva van Gloss. Reading about Subject 01, they identify her as Ingrid Volkomenn. A conflict arises about who will receive the Symbol. Joui proposes a plan, but the Gatekeeper suggests another. The discussion intensifies, and Thiago decides to sacrifice himself. The group names themselves "Team E" after the Gatekeeper. Thiago receives the Symbol and enters the Infinite Labyrinth, guiding them. The Gatekeeper distracts the Lucidios while the team hides in the Blacksmith's Forge. They realize the Lucidios are heading to the forest for the Gatekeeper's execution. In the labyrinth, they face challenges and discover cells with springs. Liz struggles with the decision to kill them. Upon reaching the heart of the cave, they confront the Blacksmith, kill him, but something goes wrong with the explosives, resulting in his transformation into the God of Death. Thiago sacrifices himself to fix and detonate the explosives alongside him and the God of Death. The team faces destruction in Santo Berço, returns to Carpazinha, and holds a symbolic funeral for Thiago.
