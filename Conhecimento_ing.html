Conhecimento

To know everything is to lose everything.
This element is characterized by discovering, learning, knowing, and deciphering, directly linked to the "Whispers of Knowledge." The colors representing this element are white and shades of yellow. The behavior of the Knowledge Entity seems to be directly connected to the idea of gaining more knowledge. Events that have been a significant source of Fear, recorded in texts, words, or even Sigils, please and strengthen the Knowledge Entity. Understanding Knowledge completely would be equivalent to knowing absolutely everything from the Other Side.
"Knowledge is the entity of consciousness. Discovering, learning, knowing, deciphering. Having one's own perception of the Other Side and its entities pleases the Knowledge element."
The reason and logic of Knowledge reintegrate and suppress the chaos of Energy.

Mask of Despair

The Mask of Despair is a white mask with three large grooves descending from the eyes and mouth to the edge of the mask. In the region of the nose, there is a slit in the shape of an inverted heart, resembling a skull. It forms a facial expression of distress and despair. On the forehead of the mask, the Symbol of the Knowledge Relic is embedded. The translation of the sigils of the symbol leads to the repetition of a single phrase: "Nostis Omnia Perdet Omnia" (To know everything is to lose everything), the guiding phrase of the Knowledge Element. The origin of the Mask of Despair or who its first bearer was is unknown, only that it has been in reality "since the beginning," and that the Mask Sect has been protecting the balance extremely cautiously for thousands of years, never directly interfering in events. To protect the balance, the Mask Sect offers agreements to people who become too powerful, thus giving them the choice to join the sect or face it in combat.

Magistrate

The Magistrate, or The Magistrado, is the title given to the leader of the Mask Sect and the bearer of the Knowledge Relic. This entity, imbued with the elements of Knowledge and Fear while wearing the Mask of Despair, possesses immeasurable power with only one objective: to protect the Balance in Reality. The meaning of protecting the balance is a mystery to many who have chosen to get involved with the Mask, but undoubtedly, it is known that the bearer of it has the ability to alter virtually all of Reality, including memories, events, and occurrences. Those who wear the Mask know and remember everything. But knowing everything is to lose everything. The person titled as Magistrate is the only understandable source of Knowledge from the Other Side in Reality, and it is frightening to hear some truths uttered by them. Some people also believe that the Mask of Despair may have existed before humans developed.

Creatures of Knowledge

Foreign

The Foreign is a mysterious Knowledge creature with complement of Energy and Fear, having an unknown nature and being extremely intelligent. Its appearances are always reported as abductions, and its presence causes interference in digital devices and discomfort. The Stranger is a slender humanoid creature, 2 and a half meters tall, with its face covered by what appears to be a gray mask shaped very similarly to a human head, with two large green eyes and a division that separates the mask in two. It wears a greenish cape, and underneath, large shoulder pads. On its torso, it wears a greenish metallic suit with various black details, and from the waist down, a huge cloak that covers its entire body and legs.
