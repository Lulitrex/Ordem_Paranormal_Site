Rakin

Rafael Knittel, better known as Rakin, is a streamer, content creator, and former professional League of Legends player. Rakin played Thiago Fritz in "A Ordem Paranormal" and "O Segredo na Floresta." Later, he returns as Dante in "Ordem Paranormal: Desconjuração" and "Ordem Paranormal: Calamidade." Additionally, he portrayed Remus in the episodes "Ordo Calamitas" and "Jogos" of "Ordem Paranormal: Calamidade." He also plays Arnaldo Fritz in "Ordem Paranormal: Calamidade" and in "Sinais do Outro Lado" as a special appearance. He also played Wanderley Nascimento in "O Segredo na Ilha."

Characters

Thiago Fritz

Thiago was a 33 year old journalist who discovered Ordo Realitas after the death of his father, Arnaldo Fritz, a renowned actor secretly part of the organization. His first mission was the Case of Escola Nostradamus, where he started a friendship with Elizabeth Webber. He then took over the leadership of what would be known as Team E during the Case of Carpazinha. After the discovery of Santo Berço and finding out that he was assimilated into the Symbol, he decided to sacrifice himself at the hands of the God of Death to destroy the Parasita de Dimensões.
