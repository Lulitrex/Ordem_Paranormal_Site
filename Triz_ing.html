Triz Pariz

Beatriz Parizotto, better known as Triz Pariz, is an actress who has played Lili in "Éramos Seis," a content creator, model, and streamer. She portrayed Beatrice Portinari in "Ordem Paranormal: Desconjuração" and Artemis Deordelin Rodrigues in "Ordem Paranormal: Calamidade." Currently, all her characters are deceased, and she will not return to "Ordem."

Characters

Beatrice Portinari

Beatrice Portinari was a 23-year-old woman, 1.69 meters tall, with long brown hair reaching her shoulders, and visible freckles on her face and torso. Beatrice was calm, always sociable, and curious, with a keen curiosity that could be dangerous. She tended to be very kind and cared about others, trying to help them as much as she could. By her side was her partner and best friend Orpheu, a large and silent urutau. During a battle against the Viajante in the Mansão Endiabrada, Orpheu is killed by the creature, revealing himself as a protective barrier created by Clara to block the devastating memories implanted by Kian. With this barrier broken, Beatrice transformed into an Existed, ceasing to exist.
