Gabi

Gabriela Cattuzzo, better known as Gabi or Bagi, is a streamer and content creator. Gabi participated in "A Ordem Paranormal," "O Segredo na Floresta," "Desconjuração," "Calamidade," "O Segredo na Ilha," and "Quarentena." She portrayed Elizabeth Webber in the first three seasons, Carina Leone and Gaius in "Ordem Paranormal: Calamidade," and Bárbara Lima in the first spin-off campaign: "O Segredo na Ilha." In "Ordem Paranormal: Quarentena," she made a special appearance as Carol in the second episode.

Characters

Elizabeth Webber

Elizabeth was a slender woman, 1.70 meters tall, with black hair, and she was 28 years old when she joined Ordo Realitas. In addition to being a paranormal investigator, she was a forensic scientist. Liz tended to be rude and cold to everyone, obsessed with her theories and relying only on her closest friends. She had a notorious problem with alcoholism. During her encounter with the monster known as the God of Death, she aged decades in just a few seconds, finishing the mission at 50 years old. After the death of her friend Thiago Fritz, the agent began to investigate the mysterious Desconjuração Order. However, during the investigation, she was captured by the Escriptas and later killed by Gal using Joui Jouki's sword to deliver the fatal blow.
