João

João Miranda, better known as Jão, is a streamer and content creator, recognized on Instagram, Twitch, and Twitter. João, in addition to working as a production assistant from "Ordem Paranormal: Desconjuração" onward, is the interpreter of Gal, one of the antagonists in the universe.

Character

Gal

Gal is a pale man with long dark hair that reaches his chest and a blindfold with a pendant attached to it that covers his eyes. He is one of the main antagonists in "Ordem Paranormal: Desconjuração," the leader of the Escriptas, and a somewhat mysterious figure, as little is known about the occultist. His most notable deeds include the murder of Elizabeth Webber, using Joui Jouki's sword, and the battle at the Santa Menefreda Orphanage against the agents of Ordo Realitas, where he allied with Kian, who was dormant in the body of Luciano Carvalho, after bringing him back in all his power.
