Death

It all begins with Blood. Blood is the flow that bathes the eternity of the Other Side.
This element is related to various characteristics such as spirals, repetition, Black Slime, and temporal distortion, as well as distorting the egocentric perception of the existence of each individual. The colors representing this element are black and shades of gray. The behavior of the Death entity seems to revolve around itself, seeking successively the application of Potential Energy dedicated to the spirals of Death.
"Death is the entity of time. It seeks the moments experienced, distorting the egocentric perception of the existence of each individual for its own pleasure."
The temporal distortion of Death ruins the carnal perception of Blood.

Dimension Parasite

The Dimension Parasite manifests as an enormous heart made of Black Slime, spiraled and connected by several tentacles. In this form, it infects its surroundings, consuming its Entropy and forming an Anachronistic Village with various names, such as Santo Berço, Início Sagrado, Princípio Divino, or Começo Eterno. This relic has the ability to infect a body with high Paranormal Exposure, becoming an extremely powerful and dangerous entity, the owner of Time itself, known only as the God of Death. The Carrier of the Death Relic is unknown, but its physical manifestation is the God of Death. The Death Relic manifests in cycles, moving through time, not space, and can be in multiple points simultaneously, with the ability to immortalize its existence by transferring receptacles.

God of Death

The God of Death is the name given to the Death Relic. This entity of Death with complement of Knowledge and Fear, also known by the name Dimension Parasite, is the entity that controls Time and protects Chronology. It can manifest in various places and forms simultaneously through its Temporal Distortion. When not carried, the Relic manifests in the form of an anachronistic civilization, which can vary in name between Santo Berço, Início Sagrado, Princípio Divino, or Começo Eterno. It is the primordial manifestation of Death in Reality, something that surpasses human understanding. Originally, it manifests as a shapeless form capable of infecting an entire area to consume its Potential Energy and the Time of everything that is alive. However, the God of Death also has the ability to parasitize the dead body of a person with a high level of Paranormal Exposure to Death, using it as a receptacle for a horrifying manifestation, with the Slime consuming the corpse completely.

Creatures of death

Nidere

The Nidere is a bizarre and distorted paranormal creature with a shape similar to a Death wolf, complemented by Blood and Fear, inhabiting forests and wild environments. It is responsible for various disappearances of campers. The Nidere is a brutal creature, extremely fierce and efficient in hunting. It is a huge, twisted wolf that walks on two legs, with its spine completely inverted backward, making it walk almost as if it were upside down. Its head is that of an upside-down wolf skull, with two bright red eyes where the bottom of its jaw would originally be.
